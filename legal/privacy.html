Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Nebulapp, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy governs our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website and our software products, including Assignee History (collectively, our "Services").

Data Collection (App)

Please note that Nebulapp's Assignee History app does not collect any data. We respect your privacy and do not engage in any form of data collection during your usage of our product inside Jira.

Customer Data

Any customer data collected is through the Atlassian Marketplace only, specifically when evaluating or purchasing our products. This data may include:

  • Name
  • Company Name
  • Email Address
  • Jira License Tier
  • Jira Hosting Type

Data Usage

The information collected by Atlassian Marketplace is solely for evaluation and purchasing purposes. Nebulapp does not use this information for any other purpose beyond providing support services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any customer data collected by Atlassian Marketplace with any third party. Your information remains confidential and is only accessed by Nebulapp for support purposes as necessary.

Website Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors engage with our site. We collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior patterns in an anonymous form.

We do not use this tool to track or collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) of visitors to our site.

Contact Us

For further questions or comments regarding our Privacy Policy or any other inquiries, please feel free to contact us. We value your feedback and are committed to addressing any concerns you may have regarding your privacy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Nebulapp Support.

By using Nebulapp's website or products, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time, so please review it periodically.
