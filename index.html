Display issue's assignee history at a glance

Details

Assignee History fetches information directly from the issue history, delivering only assignation-related items to a dedicated location on the issue page. With just a glance, you can see exactly who has been assigned and when, all conveniently displayed from the most recent to the oldest assignment.

Designed with efficiency in mind, our app caters to users who demand quick access to critical information without the hassle of navigating through cluttered histories. Whether you're a project manager, developer, or team member, Assignee History ensures that you stay focused on what matters most – getting the job done.
