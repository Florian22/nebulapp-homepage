Security Statement

Our commitment to protecting your data.

Nebulapp is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and security of our users' information. We operate in alignment with Atlassian's Shared Security Responsibility Model to ensure robust protection and security measures for customer data. Our commitment extends to adhering to Atlassian's Marketplace App Trust requirements.

Built on Atlassian Forge

Nebulapp's Jira Cloud plugins available on the Atlassian Marketplace have been implemented on Atlassian Forge. This implies operating on Atlassian's servers and adhering to their stringent security standards.

Shared Responsibility

We recommend referring to Atlassian's official documentation on Cloud Security Shared Responsibilities for detailed insights into this collaborative effort.

Our Commitment

Nebulapp remains committed to maintaining the highest standards of security for our products and services.
