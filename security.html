Security

Last updated: 24/11/2024

Nebulapp is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and security of our users' information. We operate in alignment with Atlassian's Shared Security Responsibility Model to ensure robust protection and security measures for customer data. Our commitment extends to adhering to Atlassian's Marketplace App Trust requirements.

Nebulapp's Jira Cloud plugins available on the Atlassian Marketplace have been implemented on Atlassian Forge. This imply operating on Atlassian's servers and adhering to their stringent security standards.

We recommend referring to Atlassian's official documentation on Cloud Security Shared Responsibilities for detailed insights into this collaborative effort. You can download the document from Atlassian's website.

For a deeper understanding of the security aspects related to Atlassian Forge, we encourage users to explore the resources available on Atlassian's developer platform. You can access more information on Forge security by visiting this link.

Nebulapp remains committed to maintaining the highest standards of security for our products and services. We understand the critical importance of safeguarding customer data and will continue to collaborate with Atlassian and adhere to industry best practices to ensure a secure environment for our users.

For any inquiries or concerns regarding security practices, please contact our security team at support@nebulapp.ca
